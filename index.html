Climate Action and Green Jobs Act

Summary: This comprehensive legislation allocates $50 billion over 10 years to transition the nation to renewable energy while creating millions of jobs in the green economy. Key provisions include tax incentives for solar/wind development, worker retraining programs for fossil fuel industry employees, and grants for communities affected by energy transitions.

Digital Privacy Protection Act

Summary: This bill establishes strong consumer data protections, requiring companies to obtain explicit opt-in consent before collecting personal information. It creates a new Digital Privacy Commission to enforce regulations and imposes significant fines for violations. The bill also includes a "right to be forgotten" provision allowing users to request data deletion.
